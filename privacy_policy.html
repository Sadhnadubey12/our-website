Our Privacy Commitment

At Webgro Solutions, we prioritize the protection of your personal information. This policy details how we handle, share, and safeguard data collected through our services, websites, and mobile applications when you interact with our platform.

As a provider of business and marketing technology services, we work with organizations that trust us with their data management needs. We process information on their behalf while maintaining the highest standards of data protection.

Information We Handle

Directly Provided Data

When you register or communicate with us, we collect information including your name, business details, contact information, and payment details.

Client-Supplied Information

Our clients may provide customer data including contact details, demographic information, and interaction history which we process on their behalf.

Digital Footprints & Cookies

We utilize cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our digital platforms. These help us understand how you interact with our services and improve functionality.

Session Cookies

Temporary identifiers that expire when you close your browser

Persistent Cookies

Remain on your device to remember preferences across sessions

First-Party Cookies

Used exclusively by our website to enhance your experience

Third-Party Cookies

Placed by external services integrated with our platform
